LSAT Training Program

1

🏗️ Base Training (Weeks 1–4)

  • Learn all LR question types — know your movements
  • Master active passage reading for RC — build the base
  • Read LSAC Official Prep materials cover to cover
  • Untimed drills to groove perfect form before adding weight
2

📈 Progressive Overload (Weeks 5–8)

  • Timed section practice — add the clock, hold the form
  • Debrief every wrong answer — no ego, just reps
  • Attack your weakest question types relentlessly
  • Complete at least 2 full PrepTests — test the engine
3

🏆 Competition Prep (Weeks 9–12)

  • Full timed PTs weekly under strict test-day conditions
  • Blind review methodology — find every leak
  • Peak week: sharpen timing, rest up, trust the training
  • Game day: you've put in the work — go get it
