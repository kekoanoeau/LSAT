LSAT Study Strategy

1

Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

  • Learn all question types
  • Master basic diagramming for LG
  • Read full LSAC Official Prep materials
  • Untimed drills to build accuracy
2

Skill Building (Weeks 5–8)

  • Timed section practice
  • Review every wrong answer in depth
  • Focus on weakest question types
  • Complete at least 2 full PTs
3

Test Simulation (Weeks 9–12)

  • Full timed PTs weekly
  • Strict test-day conditions
  • Blind review methodology
  • Refine timing strategies
